<Section icon={Download} title="Platform Availability: PC, Mac, and Android Setup">

*Ripples* is built on the highly versatile Ren'Py engine, allowing it to run seamlessly across multiple desktop and mobile operating systems. When the **Ripples ep 10 release date** arrives, builds will be deployed simultaneously for Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android.

For mobile gamers, configuring the Android version correctly is crucial to prevent losing progress. Unlike desktop operating systems where saves are stored in a dedicated user directory, Android handles application storage strictly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Backup Current Saves">
    Navigate to your device's internal storage: `Android/data/com.jestur.ripples/files/saves` and copy the files to a secure backup folder.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Download the Official APK">
    Once Episode 10 is released, download the updated APK file directly from the official itch.io page.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install the Update">
    Open the APK. If prompted, select "Update Existing Application" to overwrite the Episode 9 files without deleting your local progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Save Migration">
    Launch the game and navigate to the Load menu to ensure your Episode 9 save states are recognized by the new Episode 10 engine.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Cross-Platform Saves">
Save files are fully transferable between Android and PC. You can copy your `.save` files from your computer directly into your phone's save directory to continue your journey on the go.
</Callout>

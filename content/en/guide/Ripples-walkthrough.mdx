Follow these steps to ensure a clean installation and a seamless transition of your progress when updating to the latest episode.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Download Official Files">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Extract and Install">
    For PC, macOS, and Linux, extract the downloaded ZIP archive to a dedicated folder. For Android, download the official APK and enable 'Install from Unknown Sources' in your system settings before running the installer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Transfer Save Data">
    Locate your save folder (typically found in the game's directory under `/renpy/saves` or in your system's AppData folder) and ensure older saves are backed up before launching the new episode.

  <Step num="4" title="Configure Preferences">
    Adjust text speed, auto-forward delays, skip behaviors, and audio levels in the main menu to optimize your visual novel reading experience before starting.
  </Step>

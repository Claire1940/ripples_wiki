The branching narrative of Ripples, a sci-fi visual novel developed by Jestur, centers on the intricate relationship between the protagonist, Jack, and his companion, Darci. As they navigate a world filled with sci-fi anomalies and corporate mysteries, players must make decisions that send ripples through the storyline. Unlike traditional linear visual novels, every choice in this game carries a weight that accumulates over time, culminating in distinct narrative routes.

To successfully navigate the story, players must understand how relationship points and narrative flags function. By the time players reach the late-game chapters, these accumulated decisions lock them into specific story tracks.

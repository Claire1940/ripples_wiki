<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Ripples review: Gameplay & Core Systems">

Ripples, developed by Jestur, is an interactive sci-fi visual novel that challenges players to navigate a complex web of branching decisions, interpersonal dynamics, and suspenseful sci-fi mysteries. Unlike linear kinetic novels, this title places choice at the forefront of its design. Every decision acts as a metaphorical pebble thrown into water, creating ripples that alter the narrative trajectory. The core gameplay revolves around reading detailed dialogue, assessing environmental clues, and making critical decisions that dictate the relationship dynamics between the protagonist Jack, the companion Darci, and other secondary characters.

The interface is clean and minimalist, keeping the focus entirely on the hand-crafted background art, character sprites, and text progression. The developer utilizes a highly customized visual novel engine that supports standard quality-of-life features such as text skip, auto-forward, log history, and multiple save states. This technical foundation ensures that players can easily experiment with different choice combinations without having to replay massive portions of the game from scratch.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Technical Analysis & Android Installation">

From a technical standpoint, Ripples is highly optimized and lightweight. The game runs smoothly on older hardware, making it accessible to a wide audience. The visual assets are rendered in high definition, and the audio design features atmospheric tracks that complement the sci-fi setting. The Android port is particularly impressive, offering the complete desktop experience with intuitive touch controls optimized for mobile screens.

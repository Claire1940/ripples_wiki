### How to Migrate Save Files to Episode 9

If you are upgrading from Episode 8 to Episode 9 on Android, your saves should transition automatically if you install the new APK over the existing installation. However, to prevent progress loss, we recommend manual backups.

1. Open your device file manager and navigate to `Android/data/com.jestur.ripples/files/`.
2. Locate the `saves` folder and copy it to a secure backup directory on your internal storage.
3. Install the new Episode 9 APK.
4. If your progress does not load, copy your backed-up `saves` folder back into the `Android/data/com.jestur.ripples/files/` directory.

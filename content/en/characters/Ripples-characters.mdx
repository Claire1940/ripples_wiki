In *Ripples*, character routes are governed by an invisible point system. Every choice you make can either increase or decrease your affinity with specific characters. These points act as keys, unlocking exclusive dialogue options, unique scenes, and eventually, specific episode endings.

To successfully navigate these routes, players must understand how choice points accumulate. The table below outlines the primary route requirements for the main characters up to the latest Episode 9 release.

| Character | Key Choice Point | Relationship Points Needed | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Darci** | Episode 3 Trust Choice | High (7+ Points) | Unlocks Darci's exclusive sci-fi investigation arc |
| **Supporting Cast** | Episode 5 Assistance | Medium (4+ Points) | Unlocks secondary background lore scenes |
